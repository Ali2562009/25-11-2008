Hey

Dearest,

From the moment our gazes intertwined, a subtle yet profound transformation unfurled within the depths of my heart. I had long believed that love was a treasure reserved for others, a notion that now seems but a distant folly.

Yet, here I stand, a humble soul, utterly captivated by your radiant smile and the kindness that you so effortlessly bestow upon those fortunate enough to cross your path. Your presence lingers in my thoughts, casting a warm glow upon even the most mundane of days.

As I pen these words, I find myself wishing for your happiness above all else, for it is your joy that brings light to my existence. I yearn for naught but to see you flourish, to witness the world embrace you as you so richly deserve.

Even should this missive remain unread, I trust it shall convey the earnest truth of my heart—a heart that beats in quiet admiration of you. I am but a mere admirer, yet I hope that my sentiments may find a place within your thoughts, if only for a fleeting moment.

In closing, I remain, with the utmost sincerity, your devoted admirer, ever hopeful for the chance to share in the beauty of your days.

Yours faithfully,

— A humble admirer

— Yours truly
